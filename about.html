About BRYT DESIGNS

We love what we do.

About the company

We turn ideas into websites, and websites into online businesses. Our team specializes in responsive web design services, eCommerce solutions, search engine optimization and social media strategies. BrytDesigns.com is based in Los Angeles, but we work with clients nationwide and from all over the world across a variety of industries.

About the Founder

Bryan founded Bryt Designs in 2011, following his graduation from the University of California, Los Angeles. He approaches every project with honesty, creativity, and dedicated professionalism. Always looking to grow as an individual and a professional, he is actively involved in web design work groups and networking venues. Bryan continuously seeks to improve his expertise and is looking to pursue an MBA degree in the summer of 2015.
