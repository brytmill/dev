About the company

We turn ideas into websites, and websites into online businesses. We specialize in responsive web design services, eCommerce solutions, company branding, search engine optimization and social media strategies. We are based in Los Angeles, but we work with clients nationwide and from all over the world across a variety of industries.

About the Founder

Bryan Miller founded Bryt Designs in 2011, after graduating from the University of California, Los Angeles. He approaches every project with honesty, creativity, and dedicated professionalism. Always looking to grow as an individual and a professional, Bryan furthers his domain expertise by participating in industry events and is looking to pursue a Master's Degree in Business Administration in the summer of 2015.
